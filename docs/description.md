# Hano Restaurants Landing Page Specification

## Overview

### Product Vision

Hano Restaurants is a restaurant discovery platform designed to help people find the best places to eat in Kigali.

The platform helps users discover restaurants, cafés, bakeries, brunch spots, bars, lounges, and unique dining experiences through curated recommendations, restaurant profiles, menus, reviews, rankings, and community insights.

Unlike generic review platforms, Hano Restaurants focuses on helping people answer practical questions:

* Where should I eat?
* What restaurants are nearby?
* What should I order?
* Which places are trending?
* Which restaurants fit my budget?
* What are people actually recommending?

The long-term vision is for Hano to become the go-to platform for discovering restaurants and food experiences in Kigali, while expanding into a broader city discovery platform over time.

### Mission

To make discovering great restaurants and food experiences in Kigali simple, trustworthy, and enjoyable.

### Primary Goal

The landing page should primarily function as a conversion funnel for:

* Mobile app waitlist signups
* Early adopter acquisition
* Email collection
* WhatsApp community onboarding
* Community growth
* Restaurant partner interest
* Brand awareness
* Social media audience growth

### Secondary Goal

The landing page should demonstrate enough value and functionality to convince visitors that:

* Hano solves a real discovery problem
* Finding restaurants can be easier
* Recommendations are trustworthy
* The platform is worth joining before launch
* Hano will become the easiest way to discover restaurants in Kigali

### Desired User Actions

Visitors should be encouraged to:


Download the app and that's the main thing 


1. Join the waitlist
2. Join the WhatsApp community
3. Follow social media channels
4. Share Hano with friends
5. Submit restaurant recommendations
6. Become early contributors

# Section 12: FAQ

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is Hano?

Hano is a restaurant discovery platform that helps people find restaurants, cafés, bakeries, bars, lounges, and food experiences across Kigali.

---

### Why was Hano created?

Many people struggle to find reliable restaurant recommendations and spend too much time deciding where to eat. Hano was created to make restaurant discovery easier and more useful.

---

### Is Hano free to use?

Yes.

Core discovery features will be free for users.

---

### When will Hano launch?

We are currently building the platform and growing the community.

Early access members will receive updates first.

---

### Will there be a mobile app?

Yes.

A mobile application is part of the Hano roadmap and will be available after launch.

---

### Can I contribute recommendations?

Absolutely.

Community recommendations will play an important role in helping people discover great restaurants.

---

### Can restaurants join Hano?

Yes.

Restaurants will be able to create profiles, showcase information, and connect with potential customers.

---

### How are restaurants selected?

Restaurants are added based on relevance, quality, community recommendations, and platform curation.

---

### Can restaurants pay for higher rankings?

No.

Trust and transparency are important principles for Hano.

Any sponsored content will always be clearly labeled.

---

### Is Hano only for restaurants?

No.

Hano also helps users discover cafés, bakeries, brunch spots, bars, lounges, and other food-related experiences.

---

### Can tourists use Hano?

Absolutely.

Hano is designed to help both locals and visitors discover great places around Kigali.
